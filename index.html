Privacy Policy

NodeEngine Mini · LeadBridge Systems · Last updated: April 2026 · India
1
What we collect
When a lead interacts with NodeEngine Mini via WhatsApp, we collect only what's needed to qualify them for the real estate agent:
  • Name and WhatsApp number
  • Property preferences — budget, location, timeline
  • Scheduled call details
  • Instagram username, if the lead reaches out via Instagram
  • Conversation history to maintain context across sessions
2
How we use it
All collected information is used solely to qualify and route leads to the real estate agent using our service. This includes:
  • Generating intelligent, context-aware WhatsApp replies
  • Remembering lead preferences across conversations
  • Scoring lead seriousness (Hot / Warm / Cold)
  • Sending the agent a daily lead summary report via email

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside lead qualification.
3
Who sees your data
Your data is shared with only one party — the real estate agent you contacted. It is never sold, rented, or shared with any other business or individual.

To deliver this service, we work with the following external services:
n8n (automation) MongoDB (database) OpenAI / Claude / Gemini Mappstore (WhatsApp API) Meta (WhatsApp & Instagram)

Each of these services processes only the minimum data required to function and operates under their own privacy standards.
4
Where your data is stored
All lead data is stored in MongoDB servers located in India — specifically in Mumbai. Your data never leaves Indian infrastructure without necessity, keeping it close to home and within Indian jurisdiction.
5
Your rights & data deletion
You have the right to request access to your data or ask us to delete it at any time. We'll action your request promptly with no questions asked.
